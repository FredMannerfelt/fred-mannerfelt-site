About

Fred Mannerfelt writes music that moves between intimacy and scale — a single piano line, an orchestral horizon, and the quiet tension of modern production. The focus is always tone, space, and emotional clarity.

Many releases exist in paired forms: solo works and orchestral counterparts — the same material, seen from two angles.
