About

I build sound the way you build a road at night — guided by feel, not labels. Some pieces are quiet hours, others are high-speed runs: always melodic, always cinematic in spirit.

New releases arrive as “cycles” — the same core theme expressed in different forms: solo piano, orchestral works, and electronic versions.
